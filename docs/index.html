About

The Initiative aims to coordinate efforts towards building the next generation of cosmological inference and simulation tools based on automatic differentiation.

Open & Collaborative

We aim to foster a welcoming and collaborative environment to facilitate the exchange of ideas and conduct end-to-end open research.

Towards Stage-IV Surveys

The tools developed by the initiative aim to meet the challenge of optimal multi-probe cosmological inference from upcoming Stage-IV surveys.

High Quality Software

We put great emphasis on open and reusable software tools which can be readily adopted and applied by the community.

Interdisciplinarity

The initiative aims to be at the forefront of large-scale physical inference, through a tight integration between cosmology, statistics, and machine learning.
